Pain of today becomes path for tomorrow. This day it might hurt to go out for a run but it's a very small price you pay upfront for the path it is going to lay for tomorrow. It is just the first 5 minutes that are the most difficult to defeat and once you are beyond it, you might smile at your own stupidity. It might hurt to start reading something which be benificial in the future conversation and it is going to hurt so much more to get rid of the existing feed of twitter jokes and instagram lifestyle but do it anyway. Once the threshold is achieved, it will bring a smile again for your stupidity.
